### **Persona 1** – *Ann Patel, Compliance Officer*

![ann_patel](../assets/images/ann_patel.png)

Ann is a 36‑year‑old Compliance Officer in the Risk & Regulatory team of a large UK retail bank, with 7 years’ experience in AML and credit‑risk oversight. She is detail‑oriented, cautious, and comfortable working with dashboards and Excel but relies on data teams for complex analytics.
​

🧭 Story & context: Ann reviews daily alerts from the bank’s transaction‑monitoring and credit‑risk systems, validates suspicious cases, and prepares reports for regulators and internal audit.
​

✅ Needs & goals:

Identify customers whose recent transaction behaviour indicates high risk of default or financial crime.

Prioritise alerts by risk score and explainability so she can justify escalations.

Generate audit‑ready summaries of high‑risk customers, trends, and model performance.
