### **Persona 2** – *Michel Johnson, Data Analyst*

![Michel_Johnson](../assets/images/Michel_Johnson.png)

Michel Johnson is a 29‑year‑old Data Analyst working in the bank’s central analytics team, focused on building and maintaining scoring models for lending decisions. He is technically strong in Python, SQL and BI tools, and enjoys experimenting with machine learning techniques.
​

🧭 Story & context: Michel designs risk models that estimate probability of default and works with IT to deploy them into decision engines while meeting model‑risk and regulatory expectations.
​

✅ Needs & goals:

Use a cleaned, well‑documented transaction dataset with engineered features for credit‑risk models.

Compare model families on imbalanced default‑risk data with appropriate metrics.

Produce explainable outputs (feature importance, SHAP‑style explanations) for risk committees.
